Artists Running Data Centers  
edited by Davide Bevilacqua (2024)  
.  
PDF: https://monoskop.org/images/b/bf/Artists_Running_Data_Centers_2024.pdf  
  
"In the context of servus. at, the term ARTIST RUN DATA CENTER (ARDC) refers to a series of virtual machines (VMs) within the server cluster that artists and collectives use as an experimental production space.  
This publication presents a series of conversations with key actors from the context of servus. at and the communities of radical net cultures around the Art Meets Radical Openness Festival. These are ARTISTS who are RUNNING independent, experimental, radical and cultural DATA CENTERS."
